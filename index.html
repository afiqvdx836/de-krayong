Our Story

Welcome to
De Krayong

De Krayong is a cosy warung-style restaurant nestled in Kampung Jasin Hilir, serving a delightful mix of authentic Malaysian dishes alongside popular Western favourites. From sizzling lamb chop to classic nasi goreng, there's something for everyone.

Known as a local favourite in Jasin, our restaurant prides itself on affordable prices, generous portions, and a friendly atmosphere that keeps families and friends coming back.
